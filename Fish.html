Recipe of Fish Fry

To make chicken biryani, start by marinating diced chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, turmeric, chili powder, and salt for at least 30 minutes. In a large pot, heat ghee and sauté sliced onions until golden brown, then add whole spices like cinnamon, cardamom, and cloves. Stir in chopped tomatoes, green chilies, and cilantro, cooking until the tomatoes soften. Add the marinated chicken and cook until it's sealed and partially cooked. Layer soaked basmati rice over the chicken, pour in enough water to cover the rice, and season with salt. Bring to a boil, then reduce to a simmer, covering tightly until the rice is tender and cooked through. Garnish with fried onions, fresh cilantro, and mint leaves before serving hot with raita and boiled eggs on the side.
