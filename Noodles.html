Recipe of Chicken Noodles

To make chicken noodles, start by cooking 200g of dried egg noodles according to package instructions.While noodles cook, marinate 2 thinly sliced boneless, skinless chicken breasts in 2 tablespoons soy sauce.In a large pan or wok, heat 1 tablespoon vegetable oil over medium-high heat and sauté 3 minced garlic cloves and 1-inch minced ginger until fragrant. Add the marinated chicken and stir-fry until browned and cooked through. Remove chicken from pan and set aside. In the same pan, add 1 thinly sliced onion, 1 thinly sliced bell pepper, and 1 julienned carrot; stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until vegetables are tender-crisp. Return chicken to the pan along with the cooked noodles. Add 1 tablespoon oyster sauce, 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce, 1 teaspoon sesame oil, and toss everything together until well combined and heated through.Season with salt, pepper, and optionally chili flakes. Garnish with chopped spring onions and serve hot.
