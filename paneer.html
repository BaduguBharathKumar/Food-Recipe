Recipe of Paneer Tikka

To make paneer tikka, start by cutting 250 grams of paneer into cubes. In a bowl, mix 1 cup thick yogurt, 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste, 1 tablespoon besan (gram flour), 1 tablespoon lemon juice, 1 teaspoon each of cumin powder, coriander powder, and garam masala, along with 1/2 teaspoon each of turmeric powder and red chili powder, and salt to taste. Add 1 tablespoon vegetable oil and blend everything into a smooth marinade. Coat the paneer cubes with this marinade, cover, and refrigerate for at least 1 hour. Preheat the grill to medium-high or oven to 200°C (400°F). Thread the marinated paneer onto skewers and grill for 10-12 minutes, turning occasionally, until golden brown and slightly charred. Alternatively, bake for 15-20 minutes, flipping halfway through. Garnish with chaat masala and fresh cilantro leaves before serving hot with lemon wedges and mint chutney.
